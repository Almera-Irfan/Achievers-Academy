Programs & Courses

Explore our diverse range of programs and courses across different education levels. Admissions are open now!

Junior to Middle Classes

We offer a comprehensive curriculum for students in Junior and Middle levels. Our programs focus on foundational skills in subjects like English, Mathematics, Science, and more.

9th & 10th Classes

Science Subjects

Our Science stream covers subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with hands-on practical work and laboratory sessions.

Arts Subjects

The Arts stream provides students with a solid grounding in subjects like History, Geography, Economics, and more.

1st Year & 2nd Year

ICS (Intermediate Computer Science)

This program focuses on computer science fundamentals including programming, databases, and networking. Ideal for students pursuing a career in tech.

FA (Fine Arts)

The FA stream emphasizes creativity, covering subjects like Painting, Sculpture, and Art History, perfect for students with an artistic flair.

ICom (Intermediate Commerce)

This program covers fundamental concepts of accounting, economics, and business studies, preparing students for a future in commerce and business.

FSc Pre-Medical

Designed for students pursuing careers in medicine, this program includes subjects like Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.

FSc Pre-Engineering

This program focuses on subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, ideal for students planning to pursue engineering fields.
